About Me

Hello and thank you for visiting my web page. My offical legal name is Richard Paul Kazanowski Jr, but you can call me by my more widley used nickname RJ!

My main interest and above all passion in this world.....is International Travel! As of Septmeber of 2019, I have visited by my count 78 countries in the world. Of those 78 countries, I have set foot on 6 of the 7 continents (however briefly) and have literally been to every single country in Europe. Yes, even the more remote countries on that continent, such as Belarus, San Marino, Transnistia, Sark, & Kosovo, among others.

Roughly there are 195 countries in the world, while visiting them all one day might be a personal goal of mine, for the time being I'll be content with just cracking 100. Needless to say I have seen some incredible places, meet some facinating people, and have tried many unique foods.

Another interest of mine is digital art, Always hating the mess and smell of traditional art mediums, I took to digital art around 2005 and since then it has been my most enduring hobby thus far!
